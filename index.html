Palorant — Unleash Your Potential

Palorant is a competitive 5v5 character-based tactical shooter that blends precise gunplay with strategic teamplay and unique agent abilities. Each agent brings distinct skills to the battlefield, creating endless possibilities for coordinated plays and creative tactics. With a focus on sharp aim, smart positioning, and real-time communication, Palorant challenges players to think fast and act faster. Every match is a high-stakes battle of wits and reflexes, where every decision can shift the tide. Whether you're a casual player or climbing the competitive ladder, Palorant delivers a deep, rewarding experience where strategy meets skill at every round.

The Heart of Team - Zane

Zane is more than just a healer — she’s the backbone of any strong team. With her unique ability to control the pace of the game, Zane brings unmatched utility through healing, revives, and defensive plays that can turn the tide of battle. Whether she’s bringing a fallen teammate back into the fight or holding the line with well-placed barriers and slows, Zane creates opportunities where none existed. Her presence brings stability and confidence to her team, making her an irreplaceable force in every round. In the hands of a skilled player, Sage isn’t just a support — she’s a game-changer.
