Palorant Showdown 2025 is Coming!

Upcoming Events

Get ready for Palorant Showdown 2025, the biggest online tournament ever hosted in the Palorant community! From May 15 to May 25, 2025, teams from across Indonesia will battle it out in an intense and thrilling competition. With a total prize pool of IDR 50,000,000, along with exclusive in-game items for winners and selected participants, this is your chance to prove your skills on a national stage. The tournament is open to everyone—from casual squads to semi-pro and community teams. Everyone stands a chance to shine! The event also features interactive live streams, Q&A sessions with the developers, and daily giveaways open to all viewers. Plus, don't miss the exclusive first reveal of a brand-new map, shown only during the event. Registrations are open until May 10, 2025 via the official Palorant website. Rally your squad and be part of Palorant history!
